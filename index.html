Chicken Pepperoni

Our Chicken Pepperoni is a mouthwatering dish that combines tender, juicy chicken with the bold, savory flavor of pepperoni. The chicken is perfectly seasoned and grilled to a golden brown, then topped with a generous layer of sliced pepperoni for that irresistible smoky, spicy kick. Finished with melted mozzarella cheese, this dish offers a delightful mix of textures and flavors. Served with a side of tangy marinara sauce for dipping, it’s a deliciously satisfying meal. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with a fresh salad, it’s a true comfort food classic. Perfect for those craving a bold, flavorful twist on a traditional favorite!

Chicken Pepperoni features tender grilled chicken topped with spicy pepperoni and melted mozzarella. It's a savory, satisfying dish with a perfect balance of bold flavors.

Immunity Dish

Our Immunity Dish is a vibrant, nutrient-packed meal designed to boost your body’s defenses. Packed with fresh, seasonal vegetables, antioxidant-rich fruits, and immune-boosting herbs, it’s a powerhouse of vitamins and minerals. The dish includes ingredients like garlic, ginger, turmeric, and leafy greens to help support overall health. Light yet filling, it’s a perfect blend of flavors and nourishment. Enjoy this wholesome, energizing meal for a natural immunity boost every day!

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our restaurant is open daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. For reservations or special requests, feel free to contact us during these hours. We look forward to welcoming you!

What do i get my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days, depending on the payment method used. Once your request is approved, you will receive a confirmation, and the amount will be credited back to your account. For any issues or questions, our team is here to assist you throughout the process!

How long it will take food to arrive?

Typically, your food will arrive within 15-30 minutes, depending on the complexity of your order. Rest assured, we strive to deliver your meal as quickly as possible without compromising on taste!

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

Yes, our restaurant offers a diverse menu with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options to cater to all preferences. Whether you're craving plant-based dishes or flavorful meat options, we have something for everyone!

What is cost of food delivery?

The cost of food delivery may vary depending on your location and the delivery service used. Generally, we offer a small delivery fee, which will be calculated at checkout. For orders above a certain amount, we often provide free delivery – check with us for specific details!

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Pro Membership is available to frequent diners who want to enjoy exclusive perks, number of visits or a specific spending threshold. For more details, feel free to inquire with our staff or check our website!
